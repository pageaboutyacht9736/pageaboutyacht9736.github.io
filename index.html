What is
a yacht?

Show more Read more

In its original meaning,— a yacht is a very light, small, and fast vessel.

The origin of this term goes back to the distant 17th century and one of the versions is that the word "Yacht" comes from the words "chase", "catch up", "hunt".

Yacht history

A yacht is a vessel (sailing, motor or sailing-motor) that is used for long journeys, sea trips, and also for sports competitions.

Yacht history

The history of the emergence of yachts goes back to the distant past.

Yacht history

Even the ancient Egyptians more than 6 thousand years ago used watercraft (they cannot be called yachts) in order to move on water.

Yacht history

This fact is confirmed by clay tablets found by archaeologists, which depict ancient Egyptian boats.
